Welcome to Flight Delay Analysis

Metro Area Airports

The Metro Area Airports TAB allows business travelers to find the airports with the lowest flight delay rate. For example, a business traveler who plans a trip from San Francisco to New York City can use the Metro Area filter to select San Francisco and New York City. They can quickly identify that the lowest flight delay rates in San Francisco Bay Area and New York are San Jose International Airport and John F Kennedy International Airport, respectively.

Flight Routes

Business travelers can use this tab to pick a specific route they want to take. For example, New York to Chicago. They can use the filters on the right of the dashboard to select the origin city and destination city. Then the graphs will update based on their selections. This dashboard can be used to figure out the best airline, airport, day and time for a specific route.

Week/Day/Time

The Comparison shows a calendar of delays, where darker colors represent higher number of delays. The Airline and Origin City State filters allow an overview for a specific city or state. The Delay by Timeblock provides a quick comparison between American Airlines and other airline. Note: Morning time block is defined as 12:01AM - 11:59AM, afternoon as 12:00PM - 4:59PM, and evening as 5:00PM - 12:00AM.

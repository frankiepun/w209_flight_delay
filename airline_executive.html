Welcome to Flight Delay Analysis

Airport Delay

The Airports Delay provides a high-level view of the flight delay in the busiest airports. As an American Airlines executive, you can choose the "Top N Busiest Airports" filter and sort the Delay Percentage to discover the airports with the highest flight delay rates. The USA map shows the locations of the airports. The size and color intensity of the red dots indicates the degree of delay percentage. For example, of the top 20 busiest airports, Chicago O'Hare International Airport has the highest flight delay percentage.

Delay Distribution

The Delay Distribution enables airline executives to see how the days of the week influence departure and arrival delays. In the default view, an American Airlines Executive can see the relative distribution of departure and arrival delays by airport as well as the count of delayed arrivals and departures. These views are complemented by a map of the US that shows the location of airports where the size and hue of the airport locations representing. The dashboard is meant for executives across all business functions to easily understand the relationship between days of the week and their airliners.
